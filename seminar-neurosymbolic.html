Neurosymbolic AI in Military Systems

Seminar · explainable AI · safety-critical systems

This was a seminar I presented on neurosymbolic AI, especially where it could matter in defense and safety-critical systems. The main question was simple: can we combine learning from data with explicit reasoning and rules?

The core idea

Neural models are strong at pattern recognition. Symbolic systems are useful for rules, constraints, and explanations. Neurosymbolic AI tries to combine both, so a system can learn from data while still being easier to inspect and reason about.

Why safety-critical systems care

In domains like defense, healthcare, finance, or autonomous systems, a prediction is not enough. People need to understand why the system produced it, what rules it followed, and where it might fail.

What I took away

I do not see neurosymbolic AI as a complete solution to trust or safety. But it is a useful direction because it treats explainability and constraints as part of the system design, not as an afterthought.

Main reference: arXiv:2408.09224 — “Neuro-Symbolic AI for Military Applications”.
